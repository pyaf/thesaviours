We at The_Saviours are ready to revamp the current trasportation of Ambulances to a new more efficient and tech-savy level, by adding our extention to the current system.

Let's have a look

The Current System

Patient calls 102, picked up by the ambulance call center. Patient gives his/her information. Call center forwards the information to regional ambulance center. Any random ambulance is alloted the task to serve the patient. After dropping the Patient to the his/her choice of hospital / or to the nearest hospital in case of emergency, ambulance returns to the ambulance center usually located at the govt. hospitals.



Our Proposed Extention

Patient calls 102, picked up by the ambulance call center. Patient gives his/her information. Call center uploads this information on The_Saviours website. All the ambulance are equipped with Smartphones with The_Saviours Application. The nearest ambulance is identified and is alloted the task to serve the patient. The Ambulance is also alloted the shortest path to the patient through the The_Saviours application. Traffic lights at each crossing are notified about the incoming ambulance as soon as the ambulance reaches under the 300 m threshold, simultaneously traffic lights are changed to give a free way for the lane in which the ambulance stands. The same process repeats itself at each crossing. After dropping the Patient to the his/her choice of hospital / or to the nearest hospital in case of emergency, ambulance doesn not return to the ambulance center, ready for the next service if it is the nearest one.
